EXO, one of Korea's most famous K-Pop boy bands debuted in 2011. Originally comprising of 12 members from South Korea and China, EXo now has 8 Korean members, who are active in South Korea and Lay, focusing on his solo projects in China. The Korean members are Park Chanyeol(Chanyeol), Ooh Sehun(Sehun), Byun Baekyun(Baekhyun), Kim Jongdae(Chen), Kim Jongin(Kai), Doh Kyungsoo(D.O), Kim Junmyeon(Suho) and Kim Minseok(Xiumin), with Suho being the band's leader.

Their music has a unique hip-hop vibe, with some songs like Monster, MAMA, Love Shot and Obsession gaining worldwide recognition. They have won numerous accolades in various award shows. The members apart from performing as a band, also work on their solo music. Subunits : EXO-SC(Sehun and Chanyeol) and EXO-CBX (Chen, Baekhyun and Xiumin) have also been formed recently.The members are also models for global brands, with Kai being the ambassador for Gucci.
