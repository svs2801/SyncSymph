BTS, known as Bangtan Sonyeodan in Korea, is one of the most successful K-Pop Boy Bands. Making their debut in 2013, BTS has seven members: Kim Seokjin(Jin), Min Yoongi(SUGA), Kim Namjoon(RM), Jung Hoseok(JHope), Park Jimin(Jimin), Kim Taehyung(V) and Jeon Jungkook(JK), with RM as the leader of the group.

They have won numerous awards over the seven years and have topped innumerable charts, including the Billboard Top 100 with their music. Their songs range from soulful ballads to energetic hip hop songs with intense choreography. Apart from albums released by the group as a whole, each member has released a number of solos that have all become massive hits.

Apart from singing, these K-Pop idols even compose, write and produce some of their music. They have also collaborated with artists from all over the world, including Halsey, Becky-G, Steve Aoki and Lauv. They also are global ambassadors for various top brands. Some of them have even acted in K-Dramas and sung OSTs for the same.
