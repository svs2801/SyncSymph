Blackpink, one of South Korea's most famous girl K-Pop groups, debuted in 2016. Blackpink has four members, Kim Jisoo(Jisoo), Kim Jennie(Jennie), Park Chaeyoung(Rose) and Lalisa Manoban(Lisa), Jisoo being the oldest.


Their songs, apart from having amazing choreography have a vibe that's catchy and addictive. Their songs like "Playing with Fire", "Whistle", "Kill This Love" and "How You Like That" have topped charts across Korea and won numerous awards at Music Banks. They even performed in Coachella 2019 in the US.


Apart from singing, these artists are models and global ambassadors for notable fashion brands like Christian Dior, Chanel, Yves Saint Laurent and Celine. They have also collaborated with artists like Lady Gaga and have an upcoming collaborative song being released this August.
